# Pact Azimuth Indicator
A GHPC mod that redesigns the "turret clock" to resemble the typical Soviet azimuth indicator, complete with readout in milliradians  
A full rotation is divided into 6000 mils, written in a XX-XX notation, and oriented such that 00-00 is at 6 o'clock, annd progress clockwise.
Just like the real azimuth indicator, the "hours" are in 500 mil intervals, providing 12 positions around the face just like a clock.
